About the Program

Our Free Haircut Outreach is a community service initiative by MCGI Cares aimed at sharing kindness and goodwill. This program is open to all males (kids, teens, and adults)—no payment required. We believe that small acts of service, like a clean haircut, can make a big difference in the lives of our neighbors.

Professional Haircut

Clean and proper grooming provided by our dedicated volunteer barbers.

Friendly Environment

A welcoming atmosphere for children, youth, and adults alike.

Community Fellowship

A simple act of kindness to strengthen our ties in Jasaan.
